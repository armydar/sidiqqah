Who We Are

Siddiqah Foundation is an international nonprofit organization. We provide food relief, empower and support lives and improve health, especially among women and children.

We work to make a difference across three areas—Support and Empowerment of women, Health Advocacy,
and social outreach—that harness our entrepreneurial insight, scientific and public health expertise, and passion for social equity.
